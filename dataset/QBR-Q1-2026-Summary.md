## Executive Summary

Q1 2026 was a solid quarter with revenue growth of 18% YoY and EBITDA margin improvement of 2.3 percentage points. We successfully launched the Enterprise Analytics Module which has driven 7 new enterprise deals. Customer retention remains strong at 94.2%, though we experienced a slight uptick in churn among SMB customers (addressed in Section 5). Cloud migration Phase 1 completed under budget, positioning us well for the infrastructure transformation ahead.

## 4. Q1 Concerns

### 4.1 SMB Churn
- Lost 12 SMB customers in Q1 (vs. 6 in Q4 2025)
- Root causes: pricing sensitivity (5), product-market fit (4), acquired/closed (3)
- **Action:** Launching SMB-specific pricing tier in Q2 with reduced feature set at 40% lower price point
- **Owner:** Priya Nair (PM - Mobile/SMB) — delivery target: May 2026

### 4.2 Platform Stability
- One SEV-2 incident in February (database failover issue, 47-minute impact)
- MTTR trending upward — investigating root cause (team scaling faster than operational maturity)
- **Action:** Implementing SRE practices, hiring dedicated SRE engineer
- **Owner:** Azman Ismail — hire target: June 2026

### 4.3 Sales Pipeline Concentration
- 40% of Q2 pipeline concentrated in 3 deals
- Risk of material shortfall if any major deal slips
- **Action:** Accelerating mid-market outbound campaigns to diversify pipeline
- **Owner:** Ahmad Razak (VP Sales) — campaign launch: April 2026

### 4.4 Talent Market
- 3 open engineering positions unfilled for 60+ days
- Competition from Singapore companies offering SGD salaries to remote Malaysian engineers
- **Action:** Revised compensation bands for senior engineers (+12%), retention bonuses for critical roles
- **Owner:** HR Director — implementation: April 2026
