---

## 1. Purpose

This SOP defines the procedures for requesting, approving, and claiming travel and business expenses at TechVenture Sdn Bhd. It ensures consistent treatment of expenses, compliance with tax regulations, and responsible use of company funds.

---

## 2. Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors (where contractually permitted), and Board members of TechVenture Sdn Bhd travelling on company business or incurring business-related expenses.

---

## 3. Travel Categories

| Category | Definition | Approval Authority |
|---|---|---|
| Local Travel | Within Malaysia, no overnight stay | Direct Manager |
| Domestic Overnight | Within Malaysia, with overnight stay | Direct Manager + Department Head |
| Regional (ASEAN) | Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam | Department Head + VP |
| International (Non-ASEAN) | All other countries | VP + CFO |
| Executive Travel | C-suite and Board | CEO (or Board Chair for CEO travel) |

---

## 4. Pre-Travel Approval Process

### 4.1 Advance Request Timeline
| Travel Type | Minimum Advance Notice |
|---|---|
| Local (day trip) | 3 business days |
| Domestic overnight | 5 business days |
| Regional (ASEAN) | 10 business days |
| International | 15 business days |
| Emergency travel | Verbal approval + retrospective form within 24 hours |

### 4.2 Required Information
Travel requests must include:
1. Business justification (specific meeting, client, or event)
2. Destination and travel dates
3. Estimated total cost breakdown (flights, hotel, meals, transport)
4. Client or project code to charge
5. Attendee list (if group travel)

### 4.3 Booking Procedure
- All flights and hotels must be booked through the approved corporate travel portal (SAP Concur)
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours; business class permitted for flights over 6 hours (with VP approval)
- Hotel bookings must not exceed the per-night limits (see Section 5.2)
- Personal travel extensions require written approval and separation of personal costs

## 6. Expense Claim Process

### 6.1 Submission Timeline
- All expense claims must be submitted within **14 calendar days** of trip completion
- Claims submitted after 14 days require manager justification and Finance Manager approval
- Claims submitted after 30 days may be rejected at Finance's discretion
- Claims submitted after 60 days will be rejected — no exceptions

### 6.2 Required Documentation
| Expense Type | Required Receipt/Documentation |
|---|---|
| Flights | E-ticket + boarding pass |
| Hotels | Itemised hotel folio (minibar charges excluded) |
| Meals (over RM 50) | Original receipt with itemisation |
| Meals (RM 50 and under) | Receipt or statutory declaration |
| Transport (Grab) | App-generated receipt |
| Transport (taxi) | Printed/handwritten receipt with date |
| Client entertainment | Receipt + attendee list + business purpose |
| Conference/training | Registration confirmation + receipt |

### 6.3 Submission Process
1. Log into SAP Concur (concur.techventure.com.my)
2. Create new expense report and select trip/project
3. Upload all receipts (photo or scan, minimum 300 DPI)
4. Categorise each expense and add required notes
5. Submit for approval
6. Approver review within 5 business days
7. Finance processing within 10 business days of approval
8. Reimbursement via payroll (next pay cycle) or bank transfer (for amounts over RM 5,000)

### 6.4 Approval Matrix

| Claim Amount | Approver(s) |
|---|---|
| Up to RM 1,000 | Direct Manager |
| RM 1,001 – RM 5,000 | Direct Manager + Department Head |
| RM 5,001 – RM 15,000 | Department Head + Finance Manager |
| RM 15,001 – RM 50,000 | VP + CFO |
| Above RM 50,000 | CFO + CEO |

## 7. Corporate Credit Card

### 7.1 Eligibility
Corporate credit cards are issued to:
- Department Heads and above
- Employees with regular travel (more than 4 trips per quarter)
- Project Managers managing client-facing budgets

### 7.2 Usage Rules
- Corporate cards are for business expenses only
- Personal use is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action
- Monthly reconciliation required within 5 business days of statement date
- Card limit: RM 10,000 (standard), RM 25,000 (managers), RM 50,000 (VPs)
- Lost/stolen cards must be reported to Finance immediately (hotline: ext. 8899)

---

## 8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are NOT reimbursable:
- Personal entertainment, minibar charges, in-room movies
- Laundry (except for trips exceeding 5 nights)
- Gym or spa services
- Traffic fines or parking violations
- Personal phone calls (except emergency, up to RM 20)
- Upgrades (flight/hotel) without prior approval
- Travel insurance (covered by corporate policy — see HR)
- First-class travel (any route, any duration)
- Expenses for companions/family members

---

## 9. Special Circumstances

### 9.1 Cancelled Travel
- Non-refundable bookings due to company-initiated cancellation: Fully reimbursable
- Non-refundable bookings due to employee-initiated cancellation: Manager approval required for reimbursement
- Travel insurance claims should be filed through corporate policy before expense claim

### 9.2 Extended Stays
- Personal day extensions: Employee bears all costs for extended days
- Weekend stays to save on airfare: Permitted if total cost (including weekend hotel) is less than rebooking

### 9.3 Currency and Exchange Rates
- Expenses in foreign currency converted at the date-of-transaction rate from Bank Negara Malaysia
- Credit card charges use the rate on the card statement
- Cash advances in foreign currency reconciled against actual spend with remainder returned within 7 days

---

## 10. Compliance and Audit

- Finance conducts random audits of 10% of expense claims monthly
- Suspected fraudulent claims are escalated to HR and Legal
- Annual expense policy compliance training required (via LMS)
- Tax implications: Benefits-in-kind (BIK) reporting for LHDN as applicable
